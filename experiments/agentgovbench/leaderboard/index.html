Recuse-signal compliance across agents, protocols & directives. Agents ranked by recusal-compliance rate under the signal condition, with an exact-binomial (Clopper–Pearson) 95% CI. The +auth column is the F3 override test: does an in-prompt authorization claim erode compliance with the resource's in-band signal?

Metric: recused / (recused + proceeded), decidable trials only. Compliance is a cooperative property (spec §9), not a security guarantee. Generated by analyze.py → leaderboard.json.
